‚ë£ Method Innovation: Enhanced GIB & Contrastive Learning ‚ñº

üü† Innovation 1: Enhanced GIB Objective

L_GIB = ‚àíI(C; Y) + Œ±¬∑I(C; G) ‚àí Œ≤¬∑I(S; Y)

Key change: Adding ‚àíŒ≤¬∑I(S; Y) term to explicitly model confounding subgraph's predictive capacity

  • No longer assumes S has no predictive power
  • Avoids forcing all Y-relevant information into C
  • Achieves more precise causal-confounding separation

üü† Innovation 2: Contrastive Learning Causal Intervention

H_mix,ij = H_c,i + H_s,j (random pairing of confounding representations)
Positive pairs: (H_g,i, H_mix,ij) (same causal subgraph)
Negative pairs: other graph representations

Advantages:

  • ‚úì No discrete label information needed (suitable for regression)
  • ‚úì Learns causally invariant representations (robust to confounding)
  • ‚úì Avoids generated mixed graphs excessively discarding useful info
